HopNet

A private messenger with no servers and no phone number.

Most messengers route your conversations through company servers. HopNet doesn't. It connects your phone directly to the people you talk to, and encrypts every message end-to-end — so no company, including us, sits in the middle.

Why HopNet is different

  • No servers — messages travel peer-to-peer, device to device.
  • No phone number, no email, no account — your identity is a key that lives only on your phone.
  • End-to-end encryption — X3DH key agreement and the Double Ratchet, using standard algorithms.
  • You're in control — add contacts by QR code, verify with safety numbers, wipe everything instantly.
